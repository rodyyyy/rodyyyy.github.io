### 一，发音细节

#### 1，do ， you ，know

后面单词跟原因开头的话，有个W的音连读

> * do 
>
> eg：do it   ,   how you doing
>
> * you
>
> eg:   are you okay，you are looking so lovely
>
> * know
>
> eg:  i know it's hard，i don't know what that means
